About Us

Warrior & Sons works with customers from around the world who are constantly on the lookout for the best possible business partners to source commodities, raw materials and products for their everyday business activities. Acting as a consulting intermediary and as a business partner on your behalf, our organization makes it its obligation to dive deep into every posible supplier to evaluate their product offering, market position, competitors and main advantages to determine if doing business with them will be benefitial for you.

We work on a long term vision with our customers, making sure every connection we enable for them will be beneficial not only for a single one-time transaction, but for repetitive business and volume leverage as your operations improve over time.

Why Work With Us?

Choosing the right supplier to source your materials and products is no easy task. It takes quite an amount of resources and time to make that final call. From selecting from different choices, to establishing prompt contact with them, to requesting samples, to researching their company trajectory and comitment to quality and after service support, working with an experienced company that does this for you may very well be the way to go.

Focus on your business while we do the heavy lifting with you sourcing needs. With a wide knowledge on trade and an important supplier database acquired from years of experience through our network, we make sure we find the best in a shorter period of time.
